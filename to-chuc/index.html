LEARNING PLATFORM

As your industry and business model evolve, you need a learning solution that helps you deliver innovation on time and on budget.

With our platform, you can evaluate the technical abilities of your teams, align learning to key business objectives and close skills gaps in critical areas like cloud, mobile, security, design and data.

Make all the right moves

Our platform empowers your organization to keep ahead of the pace of change.

With thousands of expert-authored courses on leading technologies, exceeding deadlines, solving problems and onboarding has never been easier.

Learning that equals ROI
Use channels to create custom learning plans that align to your unique teams, projects and business objectives. But don't stop there.
With advanced channels analytics, you can track how your employees are progressing through channels, so you can make sure they're consuming the content that's most critical to your goals.
Stop the guesswork on where skills stand
Without a way to measure the technical abilities of your employees, you risk having unproductive teams that can’t deliver or keep pace.
Use Pluralsight IQ to uncover knowledge gaps, benchmark skills and measure proficiency in roles. Based on their Skill or Role IQ, your team members will get recommended content to help them build the right skills to level up.
Practice and apply skills faster

Your team can learn new technologies with confidence and practice in risk-free environments. Interactive courses allow them to practice as they learn with hands-on coding challenges and guided feedback.

Projects allow them to put what they’ve learned into practice by applying it to real-world scenarios on their local developer workspace.

Data you’ve never seen before
Advanced skills analytics give you unprecedented visibility into your organization's strengths and weaknesses.
Individual, team and topic-level insights help you find opportunities for growth, discover hidden talent and align people to the right projects to reach objectives faster.
